The Team

CHRISTOPHER LAM

CO-FOUNDER, CEO

Christopher is an aspiring full-stack web developer who helped jump-start EZPC Parts with the goal of learning and gaining experience in the general practice of software engineering and web development. With his love and passion for PC and building PCs. Christopher came up with the concept of creating a website that sells PC parts. After successfully building his first PC, he found that it was too time consuming looking for PC components that he needed, as there are various websites that offer only specific kinds of PC parts. Furthermore, it was difficult to track which parts he bought from where and had difficulty purchasing the parts he needed due to the many amount steps needed to take in order to purchase the product. Thus, EZPC Parts was born in hopes of making it easy to browse and buy products with ease.


THANH DO

CO-FOUNDER, CEO

Thanh is a soon a to be UCI graduate with a B.S in Software Engineering. He's an avid gamer and loves building computer, and thus together with Chris they came up with the idea of selling computer parts. Using his experience building multiple computers, he understands the process of picking parts and comparing prices and aims to expedite this process by creating EZPC Parts. Aside from being co-founder of EZPC computer parts, he's a full-stack developer.
